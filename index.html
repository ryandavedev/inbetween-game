Mechanics

1. Draw 2 random numbers between 1 to 13.

2. Click the Call button to draw the third number.

3. If the third number is In-between of the first and the second number, YOU WIN. otherwise, you lose!

Example: 3 and 9, the third number is 5. YOU WIN!

If you had the same number when you draw. For an example, 2 and 2. You'll have the option to choose between lower and higher.

Lower - the third number will be greater less than the first two numbers.

Higher - the third number will be greater than the first two numbers.

Enjoy the Game!
